Passion 1: Music

I have always been passionate about music. I started playing instruments at a young age. Going from the recorder in 3rd grade, to the clarinet in 4th grade, and then onto the alto saxophone in 5th grade, I enjoyed getting to know more and more instruments. I stuck with the saxophone for 6 years and only stopped playing due to time constraints brought on by school. If I was able to afford a saxophone of my own and private lessons, I would definitely resume playing. During my years as a budding musician, I had the honor of getting to interact and play with many amazing people. I worked with renowned musicians such as Virigina Mayhew, Oscar Perez, Kelly Green, Bruce Williams, and more. I performed for the Nikil Badlani Foundation annually and also took part in concerts put on by Jazz House Kids. Although I stopped playing in bands around 2020, that did not dim my love for music. I registered up for my first Spotify account during 8th grade in 2016 and I have been a loyal user ever since. Music is apart of my daily life and I want to keep it that way as I search for a career.
